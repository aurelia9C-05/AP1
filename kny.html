Info

Kyojuro Rengoku (煉れん獄ごく 杏きょう寿じゅ郎ろう Rengoku Kyōjurō), a major supporting character from "Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba," is the charismatic Flame Hashira known for his tall stature, lean-muscular build, and distinctive appearance. With long bright yellow hair streaked with red, golden eyes fading to red, and a perpetually enthusiastic smile, he embodies optimism and dedication. He wears the standard black Demon Slayer uniform, complemented by a flame-patterned haori inherited from his father. Renowned for his immense strength, speed, and stamina, Kyojuro wields a Nichirin katana with a red flame-like pattern and the words "Destroyer of Demons" engraved on it. As a skilled practitioner of Flame Breathing, he employs techniques like the Ninth Form: Rengoku, which demonstrates his mastery in combat. His unwavering will and honor, coupled with his deep respect for his family and duty, make him a revered and inspirational figure in the series.
